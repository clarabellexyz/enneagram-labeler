How it works

This labeler applies a self-reported Enneagram label to your Bluesky profile. Labels appear as tags that others can see.

Type — your core Enneagram type (1–9)

Subtype — your instinctual variant (SP · SO · SX)

Labels are applied via the AT Protocol labeling system and stored on your account. You can change or remove them anytime.
